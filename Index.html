Are these treatments safe? +
Yes — all of our treatments are FDA-cleared non-invasive technologies. They are safe for most adults and have been used worldwide for years. We conduct a thorough health intake before every session to ensure the right fit for you.
How many sessions will I need? +
Results vary based on your goals and body. Most clients see visible changes after 3–6 sessions, with optimal results after a full package of 8–12 sessions. We'll create a personalized plan during your consultation.
Is there any downtime after treatments? +
None. Our treatments are completely non-invasive — you can return to your normal activities immediately. Many clients come in on their lunch break!
What areas of the body can be treated? +
We treat the abdomen, flanks (love handles), thighs, arms, back, buttocks, and more. Our specialist will identify the best target areas based on your body goals during consultation.
Do you offer package deals? +
Absolutely. We offer single sessions as well as multi-session packages with savings of up to 20%. Custom bundles combining multiple services are also available. Ask us during your free consultation.
Who is a good candidate for body sculpting? +
Our treatments are ideal for adults who are at or near their healthy weight but struggle with stubborn fat pockets, cellulite, loose skin, or want to refine and tone specific areas. They are not a substitute for significant weight loss.
